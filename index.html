About

What I love to do is designing user-centered web-stories and experimenting with the latest front-end techniques and CSS tricks in order to give them appealing form and substance.

in other words im a UX Developer.

My toolkit

  • Design

    UX Design, Interactive Design, User Analysis, Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Usability, Printed media.

  • Front-end Development

    HTML(5), CSS(3), Sass, JavaScript, JQuery, Graceful degradation, Responsive design, Website optimization + cross browser capabilities

  • Tools

    Adobe Creative Suite, Balsamiq, Brackets, CMS(Wordpress, Joomla!), Gimp, Github, Sublime 2

  • Non-technical

    Proficient in English, motivated, working good under pressure and a team player. Love symphonic metal, food & art.
