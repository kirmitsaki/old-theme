  • The Raven

    The Raven

    Tags: Parallax backrounds effect/ Design/ Poe’s poem

    This is the result of a design exercise focused on creating a site that “narrates” Edgar Alan Poe’s “The Raven”.

    Details

    By using scrolling parallax background effects the background-image changes as Poe’s poem “The Raven” unfolds. By scrolling to bottom, the background-image fades into one that shows the raven fly in the sky instead of sitting on the fence.

    Original image with crow sitting on the fence found on internet then modified with Gimp.
