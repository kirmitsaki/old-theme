  • Space Adventure Game

    Space Adventure Game

    Game-Based Learning/ UI/UX Design/ Game Design.

    Details

    There have been many discussions about the benefits of games in the learning process (Game-Based Learning). Any teacher or educator knows that the key to helping students learn is to make it fun.

    The same goes for typing, which is why Space Adventure, created with Game Maker 8.1 Lite, is designed to make typing as fun as possible for children (and adults).

    In Space Adventure the player is inside a spaceship and must type the words that appear under various objects before they damage the spaceship. It has various difficulty levels and rooms for player to choose, and each room differs from the other in order to keep the student's interest.

    Space Adventure is a fun typing tutorial game both for children and adults who want to improve their skills.
